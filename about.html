    • About Tekvocation

    Tekvocation is a technology services and training company in Mysore. It is founded by seasoned entrepreneurs with over 30 years of experience in the technology industry, including building multiple successful product companies in the US and India.

    As an organization, our primary objective is to impart technology skills and opportunities to a growing generation of technology professionals living in a charming city like Mysore and to give them the chance to take advantage of the excellent quality of life it has to offer.

    We believe there is a growing demand to fill positions in various technology industries, Where innovation is powered by technology, offering business and operational benefits. Worldwide, the demand for technology talent and solutions is growing. With strong connections to technology industry innovators and companies, Tekvocation is able to bring talent and a wide range of technology solutions to many industries.

    Tekvocation's program prepares new graduates for a productive and fulfilling career by exposing them to extended real-world training and experience. Tekvocation encourages a strong work ethic and passion for accomplishing challenging work of high quality and value.

    Our Philosophy - Technology

    At the core of our approach is a deep understanding of how technology can be harnessed to drive business growth, improve operational efficiency, and enhance customer experiences. We believe that technology should be an enabler, empowering organizations to achieve their goals and stay ahead in an increasingly digital world.

    Our Philosophy - Internship Program

    At Tekvocation, we believe in organic transformation in the lives of our learners. We help individuals meet the challenges of increasing complexity and growing uncertainty by discovering, inspiring and liberating their potential.

    Our training programs are consciously planned with an ingeniously laid out schedule; we value individual learning over herd sessions and therefore all our learners receive adequate time to learn, explore, unlearn, relearn and acquire knowledge and skills.
